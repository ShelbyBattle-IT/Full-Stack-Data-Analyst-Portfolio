## Business Problem
A footwear brand needed a clear understanding of 2024 sales performance to identify revenue drivers, underperforming regions, and seasonal sales trends.

## Dataset
Transactional footwear sales data including order date, region, product category, units sold, and revenue.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- KPI Calculations
- Dashboard Design
- Business Storytelling

## Methodology
1. Cleaned and standardized raw sales data
2. Calculated KPIs (Total Revenue, Orders, Units Sold, AOV)
3. Built pivot tables to analyze revenue by region, category, and month
4. Designed a one-page executive dashboard

## Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** $3,260  
- **Orders:** 14  
- **Units Sold:** 27  
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** $232.86  

## Insights
- The **West region** was the highest revenue contributor
- **Running** was the top-performing product category
- Revenue peaked in **January**, with a secondary peak in **October**, indicating seasonality

## Business Recommendations
- Focus growth strategies on the Running category
- Apply West-region tactics to underperforming regions
- Introduce mid-year promotions to stabilize revenue
